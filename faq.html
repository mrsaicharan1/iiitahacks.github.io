Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible?

To be eligible, you need to satisfy the following conditions
  • You are a registered student at an engineering or science college
  • You are above 17

Where do I register?

You can register at Make In India Hackathon. To check if registrations are open, check the hackathon website.

Do I register individually or as a team?

This is a team event with each team comprising of 2 - 4 members. Individual entries will NOT be entertained.

Can the team comprise of students from different colleges?

Yes.

Do all team members need to register separately?

No. Only one entry per team is expected. At the time of registration, identify the team lead/ contact person to whom all communications will be sent. We will NOT send intimations separately to each team member.

Is there a registration fee?

No. Registration is free!

When will I know if I am selected or not?

We will send out acceptance mails to all selected participants by 15 January, 2016. If you are wait-listed and get selected later, you will receive a mail by 16 January, 2016.

Will you provide accommodation for the entire event?

Yes. Participants selected for the event will be hosted in and around IIT-Bombay campus. Food and accommodation for the entire duration of the event will be provided for team members (max 4) ONLY.
